Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our renovation services, timelines, and processes.

Kitchen Renovations

How long does a typical kitchen renovation take?

A typical kitchen remodel takes 6-12 weeks depending on scope, complexity, and materials selected. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase. Major structural changes or custom elements may extend the timeline.

What affects the cost of a kitchen remodel?

Kitchen remodeling costs vary widely based on layout changes, cabinetry, surfaces, appliances, electrical work, plumbing, finish level, and project complexity. Share your goals through the inquiry form so Haven can review the scope and outline next steps.

Can I stay in my home during a kitchen renovation?

Yes, you can. We'll set up a temporary kitchen area and minimize disruption to your daily life. We maintain a clean job site and work during agreed-upon hours.

Bathroom Renovations

What affects the cost of a bathroom renovation?

Bathroom remodeling costs depend on room size, plumbing changes, waterproofing, tile, fixtures, cabinetry, lighting, and finish selections. A site-specific conversation is the right way to estimate the scope responsibly.

How long do bathroom renovations typically take?

Most bathroom renovations take 4-8 weeks, depending on complexity. Guest bathrooms are typically faster (4-6 weeks), while master baths with custom features may take longer (6-10 weeks).

Do you offer spa-like features like heated floors and walk-in showers?

Yes! We specialize in spa-inspired bathrooms with heated floors, steam showers, rainfall showerheads, custom vanities, and luxurious finishes that create a personal retreat.
