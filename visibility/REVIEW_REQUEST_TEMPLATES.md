## Review Prompt Ideas

- What project did Haven Design & Build complete?
- What city or area was the project in?
- Was communication clear?
- How did the finished space improve the property?
- Would you recommend Haven Design & Build for kitchen, bathroom, basement, deck, or commercial renovation work?
