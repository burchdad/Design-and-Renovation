## 📍 Google My Business Setup (CRITICAL)

### Phase 1: Create & Verify Account
1. Go to [Google My Business](https://www.google.com/business/)
2. Sign in with your business Gmail
3. Add business info:
   - **Business Name**: Haven Design & Build LLC
   - **Address**: [YOUR_PHYSICAL_ADDRESS]
   - **Phone**: [YOUR_PHONE]
   - **Website**: https://www.designhavenbuild.com
   - **Business Category**: Home Remodeling Service
   - **Service Areas**: [CITIES/REGIONS YOU SERVE]

4. Verify your business:
   - Postcard (1-2 weeks): Google mails verification code
   - OR Phone (instant): Verify by call
   - OR Email: If previously verified

### Phase 2: Complete Your Profile
- ✅ Business description (250+ words with keywords)
- ✅ Logo (PNG, 1MB max, square format)
- ✅ 10-20 photos (portfolio projects)
- ✅ Opening hours (set to appointment-based if needed)
- ✅ Business attributes (luxury service, eco-friendly, etc.)
- ✅ Service areas (list all cities/regions)

### Phase 3: Optimization
- Review and respond to all reviews
- Post monthly updates (promotions, new projects)
- Add service photos (kitchen, bath, completed projects)
- Update "about" section with keywords

## 🌟 Review Generation Strategy

### Why Reviews Matter
- Google defaults give MORE visibility to highly-rated businesses
- Reviews with specific keywords rank higher
- Client testimonials are trust signals for AI search

### How to Generate Reviews

#### Post-Project Process:
```
1. Complete project
2. Send thank you email with review request
3. Include direct Google review link
4. Offer incentive? (Amazon card, discount on future work)
5. Follow up at 2 weeks if no review
```

#### Email Template:
```
Subject: Share Your Experience with Haven Design & Build

Hi [Client Name],

We loved working on your [kitchen/bathroom] renovation! 
Would you mind sharing your experience on Google?

It helps other homeowners find us and means the world to our team.

[DIRECT GOOGLE REVIEW LINK]

Thank you!
Haven team
```

#### Getting Google Review Link:
1. Log in to Google My Business
2. Click "Customers" → "Reviews"
3. Click the share icon
4. Copy the link or QR code
5. Use in emails, follow-up texts, thank you cards

### Target Metrics:
- ⭐4.8+ average rating (current: 5.0 with 3 reviews)
- 30+ reviews in year 1
- 50+ reviews in year 2
- Visible monthly reviews (shows active business)

## 🗺️ Service Area Targeting

### Multi-City Strategy

#### Create Dedicated Landing Pages
Improves odds of appearing in:
- "Kitchen remodel [City]"
- "Bathroom renovation near [City]"
- "[City] luxury home renovations"

#### Template (4-6 cities initially):
```
URL: /service-areas/[city-name]
Title: Kitchen Renovations in [City] - Haven Design & Build
Meta: Your specialized kitchen/bath expertise in [City]
Content:
- Brief city description
- Why Haven serves [City]
- Local projects (if any)
- Service area map
- CTA: "Get a quote in [City]"
- Local testimonials (if available)
```

#### Cities to Target (Priority Order):
1. **Primary**: Your strongest market (start here)
2. **Secondary**: 2-3 surrounding cities
3. **Tertiary**: Regional expansion year 2+

### Local Content Examples:
```
- "Kitchen Renovations in Denver: Contemporary Elegance"
- "Luxury Bathroom Remodels in Boulder: Spa Retreats"
- "Home Renovation Contractors in Littleton"
```
