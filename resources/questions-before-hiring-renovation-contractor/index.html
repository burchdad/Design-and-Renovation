Questions Before Hiring a Renovation Contractor

The right contractor fit depends on scope clarity, communication, finish expectations, service area, and whether the team regularly handles the type of project you need.

Ask About Your Project

Project Fit

Ask whether the contractor handles your specific service: kitchen, bath, basement, deck, outdoor living, full-home improvement, office update, retail refresh, or light commercial build-out.

Scope Clarity

Ask what is included, what is excluded, who supplies materials, how changes are approved, and how payment milestones connect to the work.

Communication

Ask how often updates are provided, who the point of contact is, how schedule changes are handled, and what information the contractor needs from you.

Finish Quality

Ask how the contractor thinks about durability, layout, daily use, maintenance, and how the finished space should perform after the project is complete.
