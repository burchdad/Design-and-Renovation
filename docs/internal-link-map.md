# Internal Link Map

## Primary Flow

- Homepage `/` links to services, inquiry, payments, service-area pages, comparison guide, and home-renovation comparison page.
- Service pages link back to homepage, the quote form, relevant area pages, and the comparison guide where useful.
- Area pages link to core services and the comparison guide.
- Resource pages link to services, areas, and the inquiry form.
- Branded 404 links to services, areas, Why Haven, and contact actions.
