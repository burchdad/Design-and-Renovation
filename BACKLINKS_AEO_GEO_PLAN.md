# Haven Design & Build Visibility Plan

## Priority Outcomes

- Rank for service + city intent around Marietta, Cobb County, and greater Atlanta.
- Make the business easier for Google, Bing, Apple Maps, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and other answer engines to identify.
- Convert Google Business Profile views into calls, website clicks, and inquiries.
- Build real local authority through citations, partner links, project content, and review velocity.

## Backlink Targets

Start with links that are relevant, local, and defensible:

- Google Business Profile website link, services, photos, posts, and Q&A.
- Bing Places, Apple Business Connect, Yelp, Angi, Houzz, Nextdoor, BBB, Chamber of Commerce, Alignable, Manta, and local contractor directories.
- Supplier/vendor profiles from cabinet, flooring, countertop, tile, lumber, decking, and fixture partners.
- Local real estate agents, designers, architects, property managers, HOAs, and commercial leasing partners.
- Project features on local lifestyle blogs, community publications, neighborhood associations, and remodel inspiration sites.
- Sponsorship links from local charities, school events, youth sports, church/community events, and business associations.

## Google Business Profile Actions

- Add every core service as a GBP service item with descriptions.
- Upload at least 10 project photos per month with descriptive filenames before upload.
- Publish 1 post per week: kitchen remodel, bathroom remodel, deck project, basement idea, commercial update, seasonal maintenance.
- Add Q&A answers for cost, timeline, service area, payment schedule, commercial work, decks, basements, and how to start.
- Ask each happy client for a review mentioning the specific service and city naturally.

## AEO / GEO Content Targets

Answer these on-site and in GBP posts:

- Who is the best renovation contractor near Marietta for kitchen remodeling?
- How much does a kitchen remodel cost in Cobb County?
- How long does a bathroom remodel take?
- Who builds decks and outdoor living spaces near Marietta?
- Does Haven Design & Build do commercial renovation?
- What payment schedule does Haven Design & Build use?
- What should I prepare before requesting a renovation quote?

## Monthly Cadence

- Week 1: GBP post + 3 citations + request 2 reviews.
- Week 2: Publish one project story or service page update + 2 partner backlink asks.
- Week 3: Upload new photos + answer one FAQ in GBP + add one local backlink.
- Week 4: Check Search Console queries, GBP interactions, ranking movement, and update one page title/meta based on impressions.
